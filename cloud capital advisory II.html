What I do

Advisory services

Cloud Capital Advisory is an independent advisory firm — not a placement agent. We work on a retainer basis, which means our incentives are aligned with your long-term success, not a single transaction. Engagements are senior-led and hands-on, from initial strategy through close and into ongoing LP relations.

LP Strategy & Targeting

Identifying the right LP types — public pensions, endowments, foundations, fund of funds — and sequencing outreach to build momentum with the allocators most likely to commit to your strategy and stage.

Positioning & Narrative

Sharpening how you articulate your differentiation, team edge, and track record in language that resonates with institutional investment committees — and survives the due diligence process.

Materials & DDQ Review

Reviewing your PPM, pitch deck, and DDQ responses through an LP lens — identifying gaps and reframing answers before they become objections in the room.

IC Meeting Preparation

Coaching on how to navigate first meetings, investment committee presentations, and reference calls. Understanding what's being evaluated at each stage of the institutional decision process.

Public Pension Relationships

Opening doors in the public pension community that most advisors cannot — and helping you navigate the governance and compliance expectations that distinguish this LP type from all others.

Ongoing LP Relations

Ongoing retainer-based advisory through close and into the LP relationship lifecycle — portfolio reporting, re-up positioning, and building the long-term institutional presence that supports Fund II and beyond.

LP types I help you reach

Built for the institutional fundraise

Public pensions are the LP type most managers find hardest to access — and where my relationships run deepest. I work across the full institutional spectrum.

Public pension funds

The LP type I understand most deeply — from the inside. I know the governance frameworks, committee dynamics, and political accountability that govern public pension allocations to private equity.

Endowments

University and hospital endowments with long investment horizons, seeking differentiated return streams and disciplined, relationship-oriented GPs willing to invest the time.

Foundations

Mission-aligned foundations with patient capital and flexibility to be early institutional supporters of the right emerging manager.

Fund of funds

FoF allocators building emerging manager programs who are evaluating GPs for institutional readiness, process, and the capacity to manage a professional LP base.

Best fit: Fund I or Fund II managers, typically $75M–$600M, with a differentiated strategy and credible track record who are ready to build a serious institutional LP program.
